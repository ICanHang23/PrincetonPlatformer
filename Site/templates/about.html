How to Play

Objective

The goal of the game is simple - Get to the end of the level and reach the exit as quickly as possible! Avoid hazards and make it to the end while competing with others for the fastest time.

Movement basics

To move the player right and left, use the key D to move right, and A to move left. To jump, press either W or the space bar while on the ground. You are able to change the direction you are moving while airborne.

Higher level movement

To execute a double jump, press the jump key while in the air. This will give you more vertical momentum, and also a small speed boost in the horizontal direction you are moving. You are only able to do one double jump before you land on the ground, so if you have already double jumped, you will need to land before you can execute another double jump.

To execute a wall jump, be adjacent to a wall and use your directional input towards the wall while pressing jump, which will give you a burst of vertical momentum and horizontal momentum in the direction away from the wall. You can only do walljumps a limited amount of times before you are required to land.

Hoagie mechanics

The hoagie can be thrown using a left click on the mouse, which will launch a hoagie that is affected by gravity. When the hoagie hits a surface, or the player left clicks again before it hits a surface, the player will be teleported to the location of the hoagie, conserving the momentum the player had prior to the teleportation. The hoagie will always be launched in the direction of the last directional input (A and D) and the angle the hoagie is thrown at can be changed by pressing E to increase the angle, and R to decrease the angle. There is no cooldown to the hoagie, and the moment one expires, another hoagie can be thrown.

Hazards

There are two main types of hazards. Spikes will instantly kill you on contact, which will send you back to the last checkpoint, or the beginning of the level if no checkpoint is reached. If you fall off the map, you will also be counted as dying, and sent back to the checkpoint/beginning of the level. You want to avoid either of those scenarios, so try not to make contact with any spike or fall off the map.

Prox

You may notice that the exit may be blocked off, with a scanner in front of it. In those scenarios, you have to pick up a prox (insert image) and bring it to the scanner to authenticate yourself and open the door and proceed.

Pause

Press P to pause while in the game. You can restart the level, quit, or continue the level while in the pause menu.
